Description

A classic Italian-American dish featuring spaghetti pasta topped with meatballs and marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Meatballs
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Heat the meatballs in the marinara sauce.
  3. Serve the spaghetti topped with the meatballs and sauce.
