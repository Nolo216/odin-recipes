Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bell peppers
  • Red onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Arrange the bell peppers, onions, and mushrooms on top.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Finish with fresh spinach and slice to serve.
