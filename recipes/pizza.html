Description

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin, consisting of a usually round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, pineapple, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it in the oven.
  2. Shape Dough: On a floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Sauce and Cheese: Spread the sauce evenly, leaving a border for the crust. Sprinkle mozzarella generously over the sauce.
  4. Top with Veggies: Arrange peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and tomatoes on top. Drizzle with olive oil and oregano.
  5. Bake: Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Finish: Remove from the oven and top with fresh spinach while still hot.
  7. Serve: Slice and enjoy!
