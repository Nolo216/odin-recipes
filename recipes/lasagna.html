Description

Classic Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food. This recipe features layers of tender pasta, a rich and savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and bubbly melted mozzarella. It's a hearty, crowd-pleasing dish that brings the taste of Italy right to your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg (for the ricotta)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Brown the ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Italian seasoning.
  4. Layer the bottom of a baking dish with meat sauce.
  5. Add a layer of noodles, then more meat sauce, then the ricotta mixture, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with mozzarella.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
